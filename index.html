Projects

BigTeamBattle.net

From 2011 to 2014, I was the webmaster and one of the competitive level designers for BigTeamBattle.net (site closed at the end of 2016, though the community lives on in scattered Discord and Skype channels). The website was designed with a Wordpress frontend and a custom PHP/SQL backend using the phpBB forum platform. Levels were designed using the Halo Forge system. Created custom maps form scratch as well as revisions of developer-made maps. Iterative playtesting was conducted with community freedback for each release. Tournament matches, podcasts, map flythroughs, and game commentaries can still be found on the Official BTBtv Youtube Channel.

 

11 Paper Place

Animated short film. Premiered on Vimeo — 1,000,000+ views across multiple channels, 3x winner of VTIFF, Vimeo Staff Pick, many festival selections, featured in The Atlantic. I was part of the animation team, and implemented most of the first third of the film's animation, as well as the ending scenes.
